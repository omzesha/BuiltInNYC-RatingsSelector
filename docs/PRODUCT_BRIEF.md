# Product Brief

## Problem

Content ratings are broad. A whole movie or video may receive a higher rating because of a few short moments, even when the rest of the content is acceptable for a viewer, family, classroom, or community.

## Solution

Ratings Adjuster lets people choose a rating ceiling and specific content categories. When a video reaches an approved timestamp range, the extension mutes or skips that moment instead of blocking the entire video.

## Target Users

- Parents choosing what to watch with kids
- Teachers preparing videos for class
- Viewers with personal, religious, or cultural content preferences
- Community moderators who can help submit and review timestamps

## Why It Can Become Real

The MVP already proves the playback behavior in the browser. The next step is the community workflow: users submit timestamps, editors approve them, and approved data syncs to everyone.

## Demo Positioning

This is not an AI content detector. It is a practical filtering layer with a clear path to community data, editor trust, and cross-site support.
